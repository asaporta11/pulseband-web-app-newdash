Simulation

This graph is meant to help you prioritize how to reduce your risk for a cardiac event. Though the graph comes pre-populated with your own data, you can simulate how lifestyle changes could improve your heart health by dragging each bar.

This simulation is based off a formula from the American Heart Association to help you get a general idea of your risk level. Please discuss all lifestyle changes with your doctor first.
